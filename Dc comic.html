SUPERMAN VS. SHAZAM

Two of DC’s greatest heroes collide in this era-spanning, action-packed collection! The Man of Steel and the World’s Mightiest Mortal are two of the strongest men imaginable, but what happens when they find themselves facing each other?

Collects Superman #216, The Power of Shazam! #46, Kingdom Come #4, DC Comics Presents #33, #34, and #49, DC Comics Presents Annual #3, and All-New Collectors’ Edition #C-58.

THE JOKER

Following the events of Infinite Frontier #1, the Joker is the most wanted man in the world! But the Clown Prince of Crime is several steps ahead of law enforcement—and he’s on the run overseas.

And in the backup story, DC’s most controversial new villain navigates Blackgate Penitentiary, while Harper Row becomes Bluebird to stop her brother from falling under Punchline’s influence.

WONDER WOMAN

From the team of Becky Cloonan and Michael W. Conrad, explore Wonder Woman’s epic journey and her adolescent struggles in Themyscira. With backup stories revealing Diana's hidden past, this collection is both heartfelt and heroic.

FALLEN ANGEL

It’s a typical evening at Dolf's bar in Bete Noire. All characters pass through, entangled in personal struggles—unaware they're connected. Only the Fallen Angel can piece the puzzle together in one grand revelation.

RED HOOD: OUTLAW

The end of an era! Red Hood and the Outlaws face supernatural threats and moral challenges as they battle the Untitled in Qurac. It’s a hellish war—literally—and the team's greatest test yet.
